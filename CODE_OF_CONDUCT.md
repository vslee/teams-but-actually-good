# Code of Conduct

Teams but (actually) good is open to everyone, whether you're here to report a bug, suggest a feature, write code, or just hang around.

We want this to be a welcoming and friendly space, so please treat others the way you'd want to be treated.

## DO

- Be kind and empathetic toward others
- Respect differing opinions and viewpoints, even if you disagree
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic

## DON'T

- Use offensive, derogatory, or harassing language
- Troll, spam, or derail conversations
- Personally attack or harass other contributors
- Share anyone's private information without their explicit consent

Repeated violations of these guidelines may result in restricted access to the repository.

## Reporting

If someone is violating these guidelines or you feel treated unfairly, don't fight back and reach out instead.
Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/PLACEHOLDER) and open a modmail ticket, contact [@LeonimusTTV](https://github.com/LeonimusTTV) directly on GitHub, or send an email to [contact@teamsbutactuallygood.dev](mailto:contact@teamsbutactuallygood.dev).
